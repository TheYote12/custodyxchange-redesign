Questions?

Frequently asked questions.

Do I need a formal custody agreement to use this?

Not at all. Many parents start with Custody X Change before they have any formal agreement. You can begin with a simple schedule and build toward a detailed parenting plan as your situation evolves. The tool is designed to help you at every stage — whether you're just separating or modifying an existing court order.

Does my co-parent need an account too?

No. You can manage everything on your own — creating schedules, tracking expenses, journaling, and building your parenting plan. However, if your co-parent wants to participate, they can create their own account and you can share schedules and communicate through the built-in messaging system.

Are the documents court-admissible?

Yes. Custody X Change generates professional, clearly formatted documents that are regularly used in court proceedings, mediation, and attorney consultations. The parenting plans, schedules, and calculation reports are designed to meet the standards expected by family courts across all 50 states.

What happens to my data if I cancel?

Your data is never deleted. If you cancel your subscription and decide to come back later, everything will be exactly where you left it. We believe your custody information is too important to risk losing, so we keep it securely stored and accessible whenever you need it.

Is my information secure?

Absolutely. We use 256-bit encryption and industry-standard security practices to protect your data. Your information is never shared with third parties, and only you (and your co-parent, if they have an account) can access your custody information. We take privacy extremely seriously.
